The Benefits of Reading English Books

As any English-speaking child can tell you, there is no denying Dr. Seuss. If you love to read, but you’re a little
afraid of reading a full-on novel in English, don’t worry. We’re going to help you get there, step by step. With every
book you read, the more you’ll improve. To get started, there are some excellent novels written in English that are not
too difficult to understand.
